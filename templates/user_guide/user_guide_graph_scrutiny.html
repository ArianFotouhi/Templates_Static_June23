Graph Details under Scrutiny

For a more in-depth analysis of a plot, users can utilize the filters menu to select specific parameters such as a client, lounge, airport, city, or country. They have the flexibility to choose one or a combination of these options to refine the data they want to examine.

Upon selecting the desired filter criteria, a new plot is generated, presenting additional information relevant to the chosen parameters. This allows users to focus on specific subsets of data and gain deeper insights into their areas of interest.

Zoom In/Out:

Users can zoom in to magnify and examine detailed aspects of the plot or zoom out to get a broader view of the data trends.

Panning:

Panning functionality enables users to navigate across the plot and explore different sections easily.

Auto Scale:

The auto-scale feature ensures that the plot is optimally adjusted to fit the displayed data, providing an accurate representation of the information.

X and Y Axis:

Depending on the zoom level, the plot dynamically adjusts the level of detail shown in the X and Y axis. This feature allows users to control the amount of data displayed on the plot based on their specific requirements.
