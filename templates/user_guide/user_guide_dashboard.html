The Dashboard, All in One Glance

Welcome to the Dashboard – your gateway to dive deeper into the world of lounge management. With just a click on a client's plot, the dashboard comes to life, presenting an insightful summary of client activities and attributes. Let's explore the possibilities that await you.

Client Snapshot:

Click on a client's plot to unlock their dedicated dashboard. Gain a comprehensive summary of their activities and attributes, including the rate of active lounges, crowding status, and more. Each tile serves as a portal to deeper insights. Click any tile to explore further, unlocking valuable details for enhanced decision-making.

Lounge Locations Tile:

Delve into interactive map showcasing lounge locations. Understand distribution for empowering you to optimize services.

Passenger Plot Tile:

Unveil passenger trends with ease. Dive into detailed plots revealing the flow and activity levels of lounges.

Airport Locations Tile:

The airport tile presents a map with all airport locations. Click on any airport from the provided list, and it instantly zooms in, then by clicking on airplane icon, it offers precise details of the selected airport. Then, double click to get full view of map.
